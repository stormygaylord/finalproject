Bio

Stormy began her dance training at Laguna Dance Theatre in Mission Viejo, California. After high school, she obtained her Associate’s Degree in Dance from Irvine Valley College. As she worked toward her AA, she discovered her love for modern dance. She then transferred to California State University Fullerton and graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor’s Degree in Dance in 2018. While at University, she studied Ballet, Horton, Graham, and Limón techniques. In CSUF’s Repertory Dance company, Stormy worked with guest choreographers Bryn Cohn, Jessica Kondrath, and Colin Connor.

Post-graduation Stormy danced in Southern California as a freelance artist, company member in Re:borN Dance Interactive and FUSE Dance Company, cast member in numerous Disneyland parades, and performer in the Teatro Martini Dinner Theatre show. In 2021 she had the honor of joining the dance faculty at California State University Fullerton teaching ballet. Additionally, she holds her Power Yoga 200 Hour Teaching Certification. Stormy is currently pursuing her Master’s Degree in Mass Communication and a certificate in Web Design. In 2022 she is thrilled to be joining Kanopy Dance Company in Madison, Wisconsin for her first season as a company member.
